About Us

Merciado was born out of the desire to make it easy to eat well, every day. After too many unfulfilling, unhealthy meals at the end of long wark days, we were fed up. It felt like out only options were quick, greasy take out of pricey restaurants.. but had to be a better way. So we set out to create it.

Merciado with the vision to usher in a new era of food without compromise. Since then, we've served nearly two milion meals, and we remain committed to providing honest food, made from scratch, delivered from our kitchen to your door.
